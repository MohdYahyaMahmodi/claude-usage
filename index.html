What does this actually mean?

Claude has a rolling 5-hour usage window. You can send a certain number of messages within any 5-hour period before you hit a limit. During this promotion, that allowance is doubled during off-peak hours.

Anthropic's servers see the most traffic during US and European working hours, specifically 8 AM to 2 PM Eastern (5 to 11 AM Pacific) on weekdays. Outside of that window and on weekends, you get 2x.

The bonus does not count against your weekly usage cap. It is purely additive.
